Track 21: Pronunciation

Vocal Booth

Written OUGHT TO / WANT TO
Spoken (Native Reduction) OTTA / WANNA

Native speakers rarely pronounce the "to". "I ought to" becomes "I otta". Practice saying: "You otta make a change!"
